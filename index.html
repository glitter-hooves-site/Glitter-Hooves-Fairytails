Our Story

A magical little farm built on kindness

Welcome to Glitter Hooves & Fairytails — a safe, calming space where visitors can relax, heal, and spend time with friendly animals. We believe in the profound power of nature and animal connection to restore what daily life takes away.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful afternoon, a therapeutic experience, or a memorable event, our farm is here to welcome you with open arms and wagging tails.
